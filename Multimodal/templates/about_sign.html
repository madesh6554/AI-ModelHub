About Sign Language Translation

Overview

Sign Language Translation is an AI-powered application that converts sign language gestures into text and speech.

The application uses computer vision and machine learning to detect and interpret sign language gestures in real-time.

Key Features

Real-time Detection

Instant recognition of sign language gestures with high accuracy.

Video Processing

Support for both live camera feed and video uploads.

Speech Output

Text-to-speech conversion of detected signs.
